## Not-claims

- DSM does not prove truth.
- DSM does not prove business correctness.
- DSM does not guarantee reliability or repeatability.
- DSM does not make the provider an authority.
- DSM does not train models.
- DSM does not produce automatically valid datasets.
- DSM is not a blockchain.
- External anchoring is not implemented.
- External anchoring, if added later, would prove existence/integrity, not truth.
